A LITTLE ABOUT ME...

I love the mountains which is fitting as I was born and raised in Utah. Growing up, I spent my time hiking, exploring and feeding my curiosity. That curiosity spread into my school life and education helping me to be a successful student. Understanding the why and the how was very important to me and led me to dive deep into my education.

After getting my BFA in Photography, I found myself gravitating towards the software, troubleshooting and problem solving. Wanting to challenge myself more, I decided to attend DevMountain in January of 2017 where I learned full stack development skills. I now work at DevMountain as a mentor helping students to have a more successful experience.
